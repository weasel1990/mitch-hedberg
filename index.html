About this project

Mitch Hedberg (1968–2005) was one of the greatest one-liner comedians who ever lived. His jokes were short, strange, deadpan, and deeply human — and somehow they never got old.

This site uses AI trained on his style to write new jokes he never told, about the world he never got to see. DoorDash. AirPods. Self-checkout. He would have had thoughts.

It's a tribute. A what-if. An experiment in style, not a replacement for the real thing.
